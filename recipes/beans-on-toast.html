Beans on Toast Recipe

Possibly the most complex and exotic recipe to create on here, this one will
require a lot of culinary nous to perfect. When done right, even the most savage
food critic will water at the mouth; tears of lust and enjoy shall roll from the
eyes of God himself.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Baked Beans
  • Grated Chedder
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster.
  2. Heat the beans in the microwave.
  3. Grate some cheese.
  4. Once the bread is toasted, spread butter on it.
  5. Pour beans onto the toast.
  6. Sprinkle lots of cheese onto beans.
  7. Give the cheese a couple of minutes to melt.
  8. Eat. It's peng, innit.
